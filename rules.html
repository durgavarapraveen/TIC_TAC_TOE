RULES

The user interface consists of a 3*3 grid, with the option for Player 1 to mark an “X” wherever they desire, and an option for Player 2 to mark an “O” wherever they desire (except the position where Player 1 has already marked an “X”).

The game ends when one of the following happens:
1. Player 1 wins when there is a row/column/diagonal of “X” symbols.
2. Player 2 wins when there is a row/column/diagonal of “O” symbols.
3. The game is drawn if all 9 cells of the grid are filled and neither of 1 or 2 happens.
